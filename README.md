# Customer Churn & Revenue Impact Analysis

## Business Problem
A telecom company is experiencing customer churn, resulting in significant revenue loss. This project identifies high-risk customers, quantifies revenue at risk and recommends targeted retention strategies.

## Key Results
- 📊 Overall Churn Rate: **26.54%**
- 💰 Total Revenue at Risk: **₹50,86,649**
- 🚨 Critical Customers: **1,068** (highest priority)
- 🤖 Model AUC Score: **0.84**
- 📈 Retention ROI (10% rate): **346%**
